=== ULTIMATE ===

Ultimate is a Simple, Clean, Flat Design, Responsive Business WordPress Theme. It is easily customizable with numerous theme options. Some of the highlighted options are two site layout (Narrow and Wide), 4 layouts for every post/page, 5 Page Templates, 7 widget areas, 5 custom widgets, featured slider to highlight your post/page, 10 social icons, bottom info bar to hightlight your contact/email/location and skype. Also has custom background, header, menu, favicon, CSS, webclip icon, and logo. Supports popular plugins like Breadcrumb NavXT, WP-PageNavi and Contact Form 7. It is also translation ready. Get free support at http://themehorse.com/support-forum/ and View beautiful demo site at http://themehorse.com/preview/ultimate
